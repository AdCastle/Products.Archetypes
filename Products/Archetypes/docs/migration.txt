Migration from an older Archetypes Version to a newer one.

At the moment this covers migration from 1.2.x and 1.3.b2 and older 1.3 
releases to 1.3.CVS [2004-07-11].

Steps:

1) Update your Archetypes and all depending products like validation, 
   MimetypesRegistry and PortalTransforms

2) Restart your Zope

3) Navigate through ZMI's main-window to portal_quickinstaller

4) Choose Archetypes and 'Reinstall' it (don't uninstall and install!)

5) Navigate in ZMI to archtypes_tool, click on 'Migration' Tab.

6) Check if your sites works
