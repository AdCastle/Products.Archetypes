What is reStructuredText?

reStructuredText is an easy-to-read, what-you-see-is-what-you-get plaintext markup syntax and parser system. The reStructuredText parser is a component of Docutils. reStructuredText is a revision and reinterpretation of the StructuredText and Setext lightweight markup systems.

Why is it called "reStructuredText"?

The name came from a combination of "StructuredText", one of reStructuredText's predecessors, with "re" (as in the re.py regular expression module), "revised", "reworked", and "reinterpreted". For a detailed history of reStructuredText and the Docutils project, see An Introduction to reStructuredText.
